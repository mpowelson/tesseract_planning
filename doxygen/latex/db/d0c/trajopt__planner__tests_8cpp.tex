These tests test the Traj\+Opt\+Array\+Planner and the Traj\+Opt\+Freespace\+Planner. They primarily check that the correct types of costs and constraints are added when the flags like smooth\+\_\+velocity are specified. However they are not foolproof. They only check that at least one term of the correct type is in the cost or constraint vector. If there should be more than one, then it might not be caught. This could be improved in the future, but it is better than nothing.
