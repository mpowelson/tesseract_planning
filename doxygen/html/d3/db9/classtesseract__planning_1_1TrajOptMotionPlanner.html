TrajOptCompositeProfileMap tesseract_planning::TrajOptMotionPlanner::composite_profiles

The available composite profiles.

Composite instruction is a way to namespace or organize your planning problem. The composite instruction has a profile which is used for applying multy waypoint costs and constraints like joint smoothing, collision avoidance, and velocity smoothing.
